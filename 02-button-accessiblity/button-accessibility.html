Button Accessibility

Accessible Button

<button class="btn btn-primary">Click me!</button>

Characteristics

  • Has semantic meaning
  • Focusable
  • Reacts to space bar and enter key presses

Inaccessible Button

Click me! 
<span class="btn btn-primary">Click me!</span>

Characteristics

  • No semantic meaning
  • Not focusable
  • Cannot be interacted with via keyboard
